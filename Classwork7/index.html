About Laura Christine

Laura Christine was born in Mississippi, raised in NY, and currently lives in Oakland, CA. She is a 2nd grade teacher and loves integrating art and technology into her curriculum. Outside of school, Laura dances Samba Funk,performing in local events including Carnival.

Laura's passion is teaching and learning. She loves seeing the academic, emotional, and social growth of her students. At the same time, she enjoys learning new skills and exploring her own interests.

The most exciting new thing I am learning this year is how to code. I have found it to be empowering and an opportunity to create something completely unique. I am also inspired by other's works, which motivates me to learn more.
One of my goals this year is to write and publish a children's book. I enjoy reading to children, but as an artist and creative thinker, I hope to write my own story.
In the near future, I would like to continue my education. I have great interest in pursuing my master's degree and continuing to take more FEWD classes to expand my knowledge of Web Development.
