About Me

Profession

I am an educator. I love kids and I love to instill the joy of learning in others. Currently, I am teaching a second grade class in Union City at Mission Hills Middle School. Prior to this position, I taught 5th grade in Albany, CA. In addition, I have spent several years managing afterschool programs in the bay area. While teaching has been a rewarding experience, I am very excited about my new professional endeavers and interests.

Goals

Within the next year, I would like to make a career change into the tech industry. Currently, I am taking a Front End Web Development class through General Assembly in San Francisco. In the next few months, I will be making a decision about what course to take next. I am very interested in pursuing an immersive design course, or perhaps a front end web development course to further my skills.

Hobbies

Outside of work and school, I love to dance! While I enjoy dancing to all kinds of music, my favorite type of dance is Samba. I'm grateful to be living in the Bay Area having such easy access to so many different cuisines.
